# Bitvavo Integration with Cryptohopper

Bitvavo is a leading European cryptocurrency exchange headquartered in Amsterdam, the Netherlands. Founded in 2018, Bitvavo is registered with the Dutch Central Bank (De Nederlandsche Bank) and operates under European regulatory frameworks. The exchange is known for its low fees, user-friendly interface, and strong focus on the European market. Connecting Bitvavo to Cryptohopper allows European traders to automate strategies on a regulated, local exchange.

## Why Trade on Bitvavo with Cryptohopper

Bitvavo is particularly well-suited for European traders looking for a compliant, low-cost exchange. Benefits include:

- Registered with the Dutch Central Bank (DNB)
- Competitive fees starting at 0.15% for takers and 0.03% for makers
- Support for EUR deposits and withdrawals via SEPA and iDEAL
- 200+ cryptocurrencies available for trading
- Both Bitvavo and Cryptohopper are based in Amsterdam, the Netherlands

## How to Connect Bitvavo to Cryptohopper

### Step 1: Create a Bitvavo API Key

1. Log in to your Bitvavo account at [bitvavo.com](https://bitvavo.com/en/affiliate/cryptohopper).
2. Navigate to **Settings** > **API** or **Account** > **API Keys**.
3. Click **Create API Key** or **Add New Key**.
4. Complete the required two-factor authentication.
5. Your API Key and API Secret will be generated. Copy both values securely.

### Step 2: Configure API Key Permissions

1. When setting up the API key, configure the following permissions:
   - Enable **View** -- allows Cryptohopper to read balances and market data.
   - Enable **Trade** -- allows Cryptohopper to place and manage orders.
2. Do not enable **Withdraw** permissions.
3. Save the configuration.

### Step 3: Add the API Key to Cryptohopper

1. Log in to [cryptohopper.com](https://www.cryptohopper.com).
2. Go to your **Dashboard** and select or create a hopper.
3. Navigate to **Config** > **Baseconfig** > **Exchange**.
4. Select **Bitvavo** from the exchange dropdown.
5. Enter your API Key and API Secret.
6. Click **Save**.

### Step 4: Verify the Connection

1. Confirm that your Bitvavo balance is visible in the Cryptohopper dashboard.
2. If the connection fails, verify the API key permissions and ensure no restrictions are blocking access.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Is Bitvavo available outside of Europe?**
Bitvavo primarily serves the European market. Availability may be restricted in certain non-EU countries. Check Bitvavo's terms of service for your region.

**Can I deposit EUR directly on Bitvavo?**
Yes. Bitvavo supports EUR deposits via SEPA bank transfer and iDEAL (for Dutch users). This makes it easy to fund your account for automated trading.

**Why choose Bitvavo over a global exchange?**
Bitvavo offers regulatory compliance within the EU, EUR-native trading pairs, and low fees. For European traders, this means fewer currency conversion costs and alignment with local regulations.

**Does Cryptohopper support all Bitvavo trading pairs?**
Cryptohopper supports the majority of Bitvavo trading pairs. The available pairs are synced automatically from the Bitvavo API.
