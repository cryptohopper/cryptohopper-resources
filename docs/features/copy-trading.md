# Copy Trading and Signal Marketplace on Cryptohopper

Copy trading on Cryptohopper allows you to follow the strategies and signals of experienced traders. Instead of building your own trading strategy from scratch, you can subscribe to signal providers on the Cryptohopper Marketplace and have their trading signals delivered directly to your bot.

## What Is Copy Trading?

Copy trading is a method where you replicate the trades of another trader. On Cryptohopper, this is facilitated through signal providers who publish their trading signals on the marketplace. When you subscribe to a signal provider, their buy and sell recommendations are sent to your bot, which can then execute trades automatically on your connected exchange.

## How Signal Providers Work

Signal providers are experienced traders or organizations that use their own analysis, strategies, and tools to identify trading opportunities. They publish their signals through the Cryptohopper platform, and subscribers receive those signals in real time.

### Signal Flow

1. A signal provider identifies a trading opportunity (buy or sell).
2. The signal is published to all subscribers through Cryptohopper.
3. Your bot receives the signal and evaluates it against your configured settings.
4. If the signal matches your allowed coins and trading parameters, the bot executes the trade on your exchange.

## How to Follow Top Traders

### Browse the Marketplace
The Cryptohopper Marketplace features a catalog of signal providers with detailed profiles. Each provider listing includes:

- **Performance history**: Track record showing past signal performance.
- **Number of subscribers**: An indicator of the provider's popularity.
- **Description and strategy overview**: What type of analysis the provider uses.
- **Supported exchanges and pairs**: Which markets the provider covers.
- **Pricing**: Subscription cost (monthly).

### Subscribe to a Signal Provider
Once you find a provider that matches your trading preferences:

1. Subscribe to the signal provider through the marketplace.
2. In your bot settings, enable the signal provider as a buy or sell signal source.
3. Configure which coins from the signal provider your bot should act on.
4. Set position size, risk management, and other trading parameters.

### Combine Multiple Signal Providers
You can subscribe to more than one signal provider and have multiple signal sources feeding into the same bot. This diversifies your signal input and can help smooth out the performance of any single provider.

## Configuring Copy Trading Settings

### Coin Filtering
You do not have to follow every signal a provider sends. Configure your bot to only act on signals for specific coins or trading pairs that you are comfortable trading.

### Position Size Control
Set how much your bot should invest per signal. You remain in full control of your capital allocation regardless of what the signal provider recommends.

### Sell Strategy
You can choose to follow the signal provider's sell signals or use your own sell strategy. Options include:

- **Follow provider sell signals**: The bot sells when the provider sends a sell signal.
- **Use your own take-profit/stop-loss**: Override the provider's sell signals with your own risk management rules.
- **Combine both**: Use the provider's sell signals alongside your own stop-loss and take-profit settings.

## Benefits of Copy Trading on Cryptohopper

### Accessible for Beginners
Copy trading removes the need for deep technical knowledge. Beginners can start trading by following established traders while they learn.

### Save Time
Building, testing, and refining a trading strategy takes significant time and effort. Copy trading lets you benefit from the work of experienced traders while you focus on other things.

### Diversify Your Approach
By subscribing to multiple signal providers with different strategies, you diversify your trading approach across different market conditions and analysis methods.

### Learn from Experienced Traders
Observing which signals providers send and when helps you understand trading patterns and develop your own skills over time.

## Becoming a Signal Provider

Experienced traders can apply to become signal providers on the Cryptohopper Marketplace. As a signal provider, you:

- Publish your trading signals to subscribers.
- Set your own subscription price.
- Build a track record visible to potential subscribers.
- Earn recurring revenue from your trading expertise.

## Important Considerations

- **Past performance is not indicative of future results.** A signal provider's historical track record does not guarantee future profitability.
- **You are responsible for your own risk management.** Always set stop-loss levels and position sizes that match your risk tolerance.
- **Review provider performance regularly.** Market conditions change, and a provider that performed well in one market environment may not perform in another.
- **Signal providers do not have access to your funds.** They only send trading signals. All trades are executed on your own exchange account.
