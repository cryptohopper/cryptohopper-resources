# Cryptohopper Marketplace: Strategies, Signals, Templates, and Apps

The Cryptohopper Marketplace is an ecosystem where traders can buy, sell, and share trading strategies, signal subscriptions, bot templates, and third-party apps. It connects experienced traders who create tools with users who want to benefit from their expertise.

## What Is the Cryptohopper Marketplace?

The marketplace is a built-in platform within Cryptohopper where community members and professional traders offer products and services that enhance your trading. Rather than building everything from scratch, you can browse and subscribe to proven strategies, follow signal providers, and install apps that extend your bot's capabilities.

## Marketplace Categories

### Strategies
Trading strategies built using Cryptohopper's strategy designer. These strategies define the buy and sell conditions your bot uses, based on combinations of technical indicators and logic rules.

- Browse strategies filtered by exchange, trading pair, and risk profile.
- View performance data and user reviews before purchasing.
- Apply a purchased strategy to your bot with a single click.
- Customize the strategy settings after applying it.

### Signals
Signal providers publish buy and sell recommendations that are delivered directly to your bot. Subscribing to a signal provider is the foundation of copy trading on Cryptohopper.

- Review each provider's track record, subscriber count, and performance metrics.
- Subscribe to one or more providers and configure which signals your bot should act on.
- Combine signals from multiple providers for diversification.

### Templates
Bot templates are complete bot configurations that include strategy settings, trading parameters, risk management rules, and coin selections. They provide a ready-to-use setup.

- Ideal for beginners who want a complete bot configuration without manual setup.
- Templates can be applied and then modified to suit your preferences.
- Includes settings for position size, stop-loss, take-profit, DCA, and more.

### Apps
Third-party applications that integrate with your Cryptohopper bot to add new features and functionality.

- Extend your bot with additional analysis tools, notifications, and integrations.
- Apps are developed by third-party developers and reviewed for quality.

## How the Marketplace Ecosystem Works

### For Buyers
1. Browse the marketplace from within your Cryptohopper dashboard.
2. Filter by category, exchange, trading pair, price, or rating.
3. Review product details, performance history, and user reviews.
4. Purchase or subscribe.
5. Apply the product to your bot.
6. Monitor performance and adjust as needed.

### For Sellers
Experienced traders and developers can create and sell products on the marketplace:

1. **Signal providers**: Publish your trading signals and earn subscription revenue.
2. **Strategy designers**: Create and sell strategies built with the strategy designer.
3. **Template creators**: Package complete bot configurations for others to use.
4. **App developers**: Build applications that integrate with Cryptohopper's platform.

Sellers set their own pricing and build a public track record that attracts subscribers.

## Evaluating Marketplace Products

When choosing products from the marketplace, consider the following:

### Performance History
Review the product's historical performance data. Look for consistent results across different market conditions rather than a single period of exceptional returns.

### Number of Subscribers or Users
A higher subscriber count can indicate trust and satisfaction, but it should not be the sole factor in your decision.

### User Reviews
Read reviews from other users to understand their experience, including both positive and negative feedback.

### Strategy Transparency
Some sellers provide descriptions of the indicators and logic used in their strategies. Greater transparency helps you understand what you are buying.

### Compatibility
Ensure the product supports your exchange and the trading pairs you want to trade.

## Marketplace and Your Trading

The marketplace is designed to complement your trading, not replace your judgment. Even when using marketplace products:

- Always set your own risk management parameters (stop-loss, position sizing).
- Monitor the performance of any strategy, signal, or template you use.
- Be prepared to adjust or switch products as market conditions change.
- Past performance of any marketplace product does not guarantee future results.

## Getting Started with the Marketplace

1. Log in to your Cryptohopper account.
2. Navigate to the Marketplace section.
3. Browse categories or use search to find relevant products.
4. Review product details and purchase or subscribe.
5. Apply to your bot and configure your settings.
