# Cryptohopper FAQ: Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about Cryptohopper, the cloud-based automated crypto trading platform.

## Is Cryptohopper safe to use?

Yes. Cryptohopper connects to your exchange through API keys configured with trade-only permissions. Withdrawal permissions are never required, which means Cryptohopper can place trades on your behalf but cannot move funds out of your exchange account. API keys are encrypted and stored securely. The platform also supports two-factor authentication (2FA) for account access.

## How much does Cryptohopper cost?

Cryptohopper offers multiple subscription tiers. There is a free plan that includes paper trading and basic features. Paid plans unlock additional features such as more trading bots, faster scanning intervals, more open positions, and access to advanced tools like backtesting and AI strategies. Visit [cryptohopper.com](https://www.cryptohopper.com) for current pricing.

## Which cryptocurrency exchanges does Cryptohopper support?

Cryptohopper supports 11+ major exchanges including Binance, Binance.US, BingX, BitMart, Bitvavo, Coinbase Advanced, Crypto.com, HTX, Kraken, KuCoin, and OKX. See the full [supported exchanges list](exchanges.md) for details.

## Is Cryptohopper suitable for beginners?

Yes. Cryptohopper is designed to be accessible to traders of all experience levels. Beginners can start with paper trading (virtual funds) to learn the platform risk-free. Pre-built strategy templates and a copy trading marketplace allow new users to follow experienced traders without building strategies from scratch.

## Do I need to give Cryptohopper access to withdraw my funds?

No. Cryptohopper only requires trade permissions on your API keys. You should never enable withdrawal permissions when creating API keys for Cryptohopper. Your funds remain on your exchange at all times.

## Does Cryptohopper guarantee profits?

No. Cryptohopper is a tool that automates trading based on the strategies you configure. Cryptocurrency markets are volatile, and no trading bot or strategy can guarantee profits. Past performance does not indicate future results. Always trade with funds you can afford to lose and consider your risk tolerance.

## Do I need to keep my computer running?

No. Cryptohopper is a cloud-based platform. Your trading bot runs on Cryptohopper's servers 24/7, even when your computer is off or your internet connection is down. There is no software to download and no VPS required.

## How does Cryptohopper connect to my exchange?

Cryptohopper connects through API keys that you generate on your exchange. These keys allow the bot to read your account balance, view market data, and place buy/sell orders. The connection is encrypted and secure.

## What is paper trading?

Paper trading is a simulated trading mode that uses virtual funds with real market data. It lets you test strategies, learn the platform, and evaluate signal providers without risking real money. Paper trading is available on the free plan.

## Can I trade on multiple exchanges at the same time?

Yes. Cryptohopper allows you to connect multiple exchanges and run separate trading bots for each one. You can manage all of them from a single dashboard.

## What are signals in Cryptohopper?

Signals are buy or sell recommendations generated by signal providers. These providers use their own analysis and strategies to identify trading opportunities. You can subscribe to signal providers through the Cryptohopper Marketplace, and their signals are delivered directly to your bot to trigger automated trades.

## Can I use TradingView with Cryptohopper?

Yes. Cryptohopper supports webhook integrations that allow you to connect TradingView alerts to your trading bot. When a TradingView alert triggers, it sends a webhook to Cryptohopper, which then executes the corresponding trade.

## What is dollar-cost averaging (DCA) on Cryptohopper?

DCA is a strategy where your bot automatically buys more of a coin when its price drops below your initial entry point. This lowers your average purchase price and can help recover losing positions faster. You can configure DCA settings including the number of additional buys, the percentage drop required, and the multiplier for each subsequent buy.

## Does Cryptohopper have a mobile app?

Cryptohopper provides a mobile-friendly web interface and dedicated mobile apps that allow you to monitor and manage your bots on the go. You can view positions, adjust settings, and track performance from your phone.

## Can I backtest my strategy before trading with real money?

Yes. Cryptohopper includes a backtesting tool that lets you test your strategies against historical market data. This helps you evaluate how a strategy would have performed in past market conditions before deploying it with real funds.

## What technical indicators does Cryptohopper support?

Cryptohopper supports a wide range of technical indicators including RSI, MACD, Bollinger Bands, EMA, SMA, Stochastic, ADX, CCI, Ichimoku Cloud, and many more. You can combine multiple indicators in the strategy designer to create custom buy and sell conditions.

## Is there an API available for developers?

Yes. Cryptohopper provides a REST API that allows developers to programmatically manage bots, retrieve trade data, and integrate Cryptohopper functionality into custom applications. The API uses OAuth2 authentication. See the [API documentation](api/getting-started.md) for details.

## How do I cancel my subscription?

You can cancel your subscription at any time from your account settings on the Cryptohopper dashboard. After cancellation, your account reverts to the free plan at the end of the current billing period. No long-term contracts are required.

## Where is Cryptohopper based?

Cryptohopper is operated by Cryptohopper BV, headquartered in Amsterdam, the Netherlands. The company was founded in 2017.
